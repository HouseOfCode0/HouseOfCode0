About Me

The still is from the movie "The Social Network," where Eduardo pens down the Elo algorithm used to rate chess players. I was fascinated by how a few lines of algebraic expressions could be transformed into a precise problem-solving instruction set. I still remember this movie as my first source of inspiration in the world of technology. It was my initial introduction to the fast-paced world of startups, entrepreneurship, and technology. It motivated me and made me think about the impact that a small group of individuals can have on the world by building something groundbreaking and revolutionizing the world around us. Driven by this curiosity, I was propelled into the world of software strategy, creation, and change.
